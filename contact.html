Common Questions for Clarity

What happens on the diagnostic call?

We'll spend 30 minutes auditing your current GTM setup. We'll ask about your ICP, sales process, tech stack, team structure, and key metrics. You'll walk away with at least 3 specific insights about what's broken and how to fix it—whether we work together or not.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, it's really free. No catch. We're operators who believe in providing value first. If we're a good fit, we'll propose working together. If not, you still keep the insights. We don't do high-pressure sales.

Who is this for?

B2B companies doing $2M-$50M in revenue who are stuck scaling. If you're experiencing pipeline paralysis, team misalignment, or unsustainable growth, we can help. We work best with companies that value execution over theory.

How long does an engagement typically last?

8-12 weeks to build and deploy your GTM Operating System. Then we establish continuous optimization loops. Unlike consultants who disappear after delivering a deck, we build systems that evolve with you.

What's your pricing model?

Every engagement is custom-scoped based on your specific needs. We'll discuss pricing on the diagnostic call if we're a fit. Our metric is your revenue growth, not hours billed.
